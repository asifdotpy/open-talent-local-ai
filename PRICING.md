## üìà Revenue Projection Model

### Scenario: 1,000 Active Users (Year 1)

| Tier | Users | % | Monthly Rev/User | Total Monthly | Annual |
|------|-------|---|------------------|---------------|--------|
| **Free** | 600 | 60% | $0 | $0 | $0 |
| **Starter (PAYG)** | 250 | 25% | $10 | $2,500 | $30,000 |
| **Professional** | 140 | 14% | $64 | $8,960 | $107,520 |
| **Enterprise** | 10 | 1% | $500 | $5,000 | $60,000 |
| **Total** | **1,000** | **100%** | - | **$16,460** | **$197,520** |

### Growth Assumptions

- **Free tier (60%):** Acquisition funnel, trial users
- **Starter conversion (25%):** Convert after 50 free reveals exhausted
- **Professional upgrade (14%):** Agencies with 5+ recruiters
- **Enterprise (1%):** Large orgs with 50+ recruiters

### Breakeven Analysis

**Annual Costs:**
- Development: $120,000 (2 full-time developers)
- Infrastructure: $12,000 (AWS, domain, CDN, APIs)
- Marketing: $24,000 (content marketing, SEO, ads)
- **Total Annual Cost:** $156,000

**Breakeven Point:** ~800 paid users  
**Profit Margin at 1,000 users:** 25% ($197K revenue - $156K cost = $41K profit)

### 3-Year Revenue Projection

| Year | Users | Annual Revenue | Annual Cost | Profit | Margin |
|------|-------|----------------|-------------|--------|--------|
| **Year 1** | 1,000 | $197,520 | $156,000 | $41,520 | 21% |
| **Year 2** | 3,000 | $592,560 | $220,000 | $372,560 | 63% |
| **Year 3** | 8,000 | $1,580,160 | $350,000 | $1,230,160 | 78% |

**Assumptions:**
- 200% YoY user growth (Year 1 ‚Üí Year 2)
